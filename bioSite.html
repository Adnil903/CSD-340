The Highlife of Crusing

Cindy is now platium level cruising and plans on continuing the hobby. She frequents Carnival who uses a membership program called VIFP. Unlike rival Royal Caribbean who does not provide a free cruise to loyalty club members who travels with them frequently. However, the VIFP Club, as Carnival's loyalty program is known, offers several useful privileges at its higher tiers, including as priority access to many onboard venues and services like that of free onboard laundry. If you're wondering, VIFP stands for "very important fun person," a lighthearted reference to Carnival's moniker as the "fun ship" line. The VIFP Club's is really straightforward as you get one point for every day you sail on a Carnival ship. So 3 points are awarded for a 3-night cruise. You'll earn 7 points if you go on a 7-night cruise. As opposed to certain lines, you won't have to worry about different earning levels for different cabin grades or different sorts of bookings.
